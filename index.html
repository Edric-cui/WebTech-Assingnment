Web of Things allows you to connect everyday objects to your devices. Keep connected. Take advantage of Web of Things.

With the internet connecting and storing information from more and more devices and systems, Web of Things better integrates these connections and allows for the analysis of the information created in the growing connections online. The more sharing of systems and information, the better able we are to prepare for and improve our tomorrow. Whether it pertains to traffic, energy or purchases, Web of Things can improve our lives.

物联网就是物物相连的互联网。它让所有具有独立功能的普物体互相连通，组成网络。

随着接入物联网的信息和设备的增加，物联网的联通性和信息处理能力也随之提升。增加系统和信息共享，能让我们更好地迎接未来。交通、能源和消费市场，都将得益于物联网。
